enum QgsRasterProjector::Precision

Precison defines if each pixel is reprojected or approximate reprojection based on an approximation matrix of reprojected points is used.

Enumerator
Approximate 

Approximate (default), fast but possibly inaccurate.

Exact 

Exact, precise but slow.
